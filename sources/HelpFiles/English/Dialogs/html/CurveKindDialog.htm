Kind of curve

You have now to decide what kind of graph you want to recognize : a continuous or a discrete graph. This two kind of graph are handled differently.

Continuous curve

A continuous curve is composed with one or more parts, of juxtaposed points which can be recognized automatically by CurveUnscan from one or more initial points.

Discrete curve (scattered points)

A discrete curve is composed with scattered points. Each of them have to be identified by the user (a click on them).
