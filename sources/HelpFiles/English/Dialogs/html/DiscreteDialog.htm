Points picking

To identify the points of your discrete curve, you must click on each of them. The coordinates of those point will appear in the "Points picking" list in the chronological order.

You can sort them with their X value ascending order by clicking on the "Sort" button.

You can delete one or several points by selecting them in the list then clicking on the "Delete" button.

Curve name

This is the name of the curve as it will appear in the output file.
